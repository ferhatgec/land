gech scale (ºg, ºG)

    a temperature scale has been based on -13ºG for the freezing point and 7ºG for the boiling point of water.

    formula

      (celcius * 1/5) - 13

      (((fahrenheit - 32) * 5/9) * 1/5) - 13

    mixfix notation

      (celcius 1/5 *) 13 -

      (((fahrenheit 32 -) 5/9 *) 1/5 *) 13 -
